--How to make Tea--

-Ingredients required for making tea :

  1. Milk
  2. Water
  3. Suger
  4. Tea Leaves

-QUANTITY OF INGREDIENTS-

Milk
half cup
Water
Half Cup
Suger
Two Spoons
Tea Leaves
Half Spoon of Tea Leaves

Here is the procedure : Firstly take a pan and boil half cup of water in it . While boiling add the Tea Leaves in it . After that add the milk and sugar in it , boil it for some time and then put it into a Cup using filter . "CHAI PEELO GUYS"
